Site Name: Donald Graffiti

This name represents my artist name as a painter and also the official name of my art studio.

Site Objective:

The site provides a platform for describing and presenting various types of artworks through photo galleries, as well as a brief description of the field.

Scenarios:

  • At what age did you create your first artwork that truly caught the public's attention?
  • How can I place an order for a specific artwork on the site?

Color Scheme:

The site colors will primarily follow a simple color scheme:

  • Blue for headings: #336699
  • Black for paragraphs: #000000
  • Gray for backgrounds: rgb(224, 222, 222)

Typography:

The fonts used will be:

  • Arial Black for headings
  • Times New Roman for paragraphs and descriptions

Several other fonts may be used for specific sections as needed.
