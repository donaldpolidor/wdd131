3D MODEL

The presentation of physical 3D architectural models, made from Bristol board, offers a tangible, interactive approach to visualizing and evaluating architectural projects. Made from lightweight yet robust materials such as Bristol board, these models are built precisely to scale, faithfully reproducing architectural details and building proportions. They enable architects and customers to physically manipulate the model, appreciating perspectives and volumes from different angles, facilitating spatial understanding and visual impact analysis.
